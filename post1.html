Almond flour pancake

  • 20g ground almonds
  • 1 medium egg
  • 10ml almond milk
  • 1/4 tsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp honey (optional) Start with honey if needed and reduce over time.
Provides 1 serving

Instructions

In a jug, mix all ingredients together using a fork. Using a small frying pan melt the coconut oil on a low heat. Tip the mixture into the heated pan, as the mixture is thick, you may need to use a spatula to spread it around the pan. Cook gently for a few minutes before turning the pancake. If you prefer thinner pancake just add more almond milk to thin the mixture and cook 2 thinner pancakes.

Add the topping of your choice, you could try: Sliced banana or mixed berries with greek yogurt, coconut cream or coconut yogurt.

Nutritional info (before toppings added)

  • 218 calories
  • 11g protein
  • 17g fat
  • 3g carbohydrates
