About Me

In an industry obsessed with body image, the focus for me is not how we look but how we feel.

I’m Liz, fitness fanatic, nutritional literature junkie and self-confessed chocoholic!

I am also an aspiring Health coach. I am not a Doctor neither do I hold a degree in Nutrition, however, like many it has been my personal journey and struggle with health issues of my own and that of my children’s that has lead me to the where I am today, a stronger, healthy more balanced (for the most part) Mum of 3.

I am a qualified Advanced Sport and Exercise Nutritional Advisor and Personal Trainer currently studying Nutritional Therapy with a passion for helping others understand how to fuel their body correctly to reach their full potential. Not only for sport and exercise but for health and vitality.

I have eliminated the Irritable Bowel Syndrome I have struggled with for over 20 years. The bloating and abdominal pain are long gone and I no longer suffer from the energy slumps and mood swings that accompany it.

Through nutritional changes and exercise, I have eliminated the Irritable Bowel Syndrome I have struggled with for over 20 years. The bloating and abdominal pain are long gone and I no longer suffer from the energy slumps and mood swings that accompany it. I have also gained control over many of the symptoms that come with having PCOS (polycystic Ovarian Syndrome) and Hashimoto's Thyroiditis, all of this without the need for the conventional medicine I was once reliant upon. I am not against conventional medicine, I just found myself in a situation where I was dealing with the side effects of the medication I was taking to control the conditions. There had to be another way. So I decided that instead of treating the symptoms of my health issues with medication, I would focus on nurturing my body through good nutrition. I scoured the internet for information and read many books and along the way I learnt to listen to my body and recognise what it responds well to and more importantly, what it does not like. It took time and patience, but I was worthy of that investment.

So today I am symptom-free, energised and healthy. I believe it’s all in the balance, eating wholesome nutrient dense foods to fuel my workouts and feel great with room to enjoy the treats I love!
