About Me

In a society dominated by body image, the focus for me is not how I look but how I feel.

I’m Liz, a Nutritional Coach and Personal Trainer. Lover of exercise, discovering new recipes and eating dark chocolate!

I am also an aspiring Health coach. I am not a Doctor neither do I hold a degree in Nutrition, however, like many it has been my personal experience and struggle with health issues that has lead me to the where I am today, a stronger, healthy and more balanced Mum of 3.

Through nutritional changes and exercise, I have eliminated the Irritable Bowel Syndrome I have struggled with for over 20 years. The bloating and abdominal pain are gone and I no longer suffer from the energy slumps that accompanied it. I have also gained control over many of the symptoms that come with having PCOS (Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome) and no longer take medication.

So today I am symptom-free, feel energised and healthy. I believe it’s all in the balance, eating wholesome nutrient dense foods to fuel my workouts with room to enjoy the treats I love!

I am a qualified Advanced Sport and Exercise Nutritional Advisor currently studying Nutritional Therapy. My passion is for helping others to understand how to fuel their body correctly to reach their full potential, not only for sport and exercise but for health and vitality.
